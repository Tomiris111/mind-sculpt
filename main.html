Ready to unravel the enigma?

Just like the newspaper is structured by the story placement, so your mind is structured by what is prioritized relative to what. The front page is just one place where a mental state can appear in our attention.

Previous Next
The priority structure framework shows how to integrate attention with other aspects of our mental life. How is seeing related to visual attention? Or thought to intellectual attention?
What more is there to the whole than its parts? We get a clue by thinking about how attention involves a comparison. Attention as a spotlight that illuminates one part of a scene more than others; or as a limited resource or capacity that at each moment feeds one aspect of the mind more than others.
This gives us the skeletal version of the priority structure framework: attention is constituted by a structure of the mind, where mental states are related to each other. The fundamental notion is that relation. The priority relation.
